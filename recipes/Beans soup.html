Beans soup

The

Ingredient

  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 1 tablespoon (17 g) of salt
  • 1 tablespoon (6 g) of pepper
  • 2 14-oz (400-g) cans of beans, such as black or cannellini beans
  • 2 cups (470 ml) of liquid stock, like chicken or vegetable
  • 2 red or green bell peppers (optional)

Steps for making Beans Soup

  1. Heat some olive oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat
  2. Dice onions and garlic and add them to the pot
  3. Chop up some bell peppers for a spicier soup
  4. Chop up some bell peppers for a spicier soup
  5. Cook the vegetables for 10 to 15 minutes, or until they are fragrant
  6. Add salt, pepper, and any other seasonings you like
  7. Serve the soup
